Steam keys

Over the years, I have bought a number of Humble Bundles and as a consequence have amassed quite the collection of Steam keys for games I already own or am uninterested in. These keys are of no use to anyone just sitting on my computer, so I am happy to give them away. Since some of these are many years old, I cannot guarantee they all still work, but most should. Below is a list of games I have keys for, in no particular order except that I've highlighted the ones I think are most interesting.

If you are someone I know, feel free to contact me to ask for one or more of these keys. I do not mind if you pass the key on to someone else, or leave the game unplayed in your library, so no pressure there. I only ask that you do not sell the keys; if I wanted these to be sold I'd do that myself :)
